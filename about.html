Run, Lift, Stretch

Run, Lift, Stretch

Welcome to my world of strength and empowerment! I'm Rana Odeh, a passionate advocate for women's weightlifting. My journey began three years ago when I first picked up a dumbbell and felt the thrill of my own growing strength. Ever since, I've been on a mission to encourage women to explore their own power through weightlifting. It's not just about the numbers on the plates; it's about the stories, the struggles, and the victories that come with every lift. Weightlifting is more than a physical pursuit; it's a transformative journey that builds not only muscles but also confidence and resilience. Through this website, I aim to share the knowledge I've gained from years of dedicated practice and research. Join me in celebrating the strength of women in weightlifting, as we lift not just weights, but each other's spirits and aspirations.
